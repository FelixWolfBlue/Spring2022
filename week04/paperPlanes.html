  1. Fold the paper in half vertically.
  2. Unfold the paper and fold the top corners towards the center.
  3. Fold the edges along the center line.
  4. Fold the plane in half towards you.
  5. Fold the wings down, matching the edges on the top with the edges on the bottom.
  6. Use glue or tape on the inside to avoid unfolding mid-flight.
