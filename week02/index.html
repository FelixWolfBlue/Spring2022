The real question is, what isn't there to love about Minecraft?

Let's do a top 10 list. Those are popular these days, right?

Top 10 Reasons Minecraft BODIES every other game out there.

  1. Minecraft has the fun of playing with Legos, going outside, and playing video games all in one.
  2. Minecraft has an endless amount of opportunities to create and explore.
  3. Minecraft is cheap compared to other AAA games out there. (Just $20 vs $60)
  4. Minecraft has a fantastic online community. You can play with friends or make new ones if you'd like.
  5. Minecraft is a base game that can be modified to play almost any other game imaginable.
